airbnbs

Airbnbs are the most practical and economic way to stay in Tulum as a group. There's hundreds of beautiful condos and villas online that hold up to 16 people. I would recommend condos in the newly developed neighborhood of Aldea Zama which is about a 6 minute drive or short bike ride to the beaches. There's also shops and restaurants in the Aldea Zama area. It's great if you're staying here to rent a car.

Aldea Zama

Aldea Zama is a wonderful neighborhood comprised of luxury condos and hotels. Click here or the photo to the left to check out our Airbnb suggestions!


Tulum City Center

Staying in the city of Tulum is also a great option! It's a bit busier than the rest of Tulum but offers a great night life and restaurants.
