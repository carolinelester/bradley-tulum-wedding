rental cars

The easiest way to get around Tulum is definitely by car. At times parking can be difficult and traffic heavy near the hotel zone but renting a car in Mexico can be cheap and easy! We recommend getting a 4x4 to take on any difficult unpaved roads you might encounter.

in cancun

When renting in Cancun its important to work with local rental companies and not big names as they perform countless scams. We recommend Easy Way Rental Car that's a local rental companies with no frills or scams. It might appear cheaper to rent from a "big name company" but they have a habit of large hidden fees and taking advantage of tourist. You can rent a car with insurance at Easy Way for around $20-30 per day.

in tulum

If you'd like to take a shuttle or taxi to Tulum but take a day to explore some of the cenotes or check out Chichen Itza, it is very easy to rent a car in Tulum. It can also be cheaper than renting outside of the cancun airport. We recommend Mex Rental Car in Tulum which normally cost $20-30 per day.
