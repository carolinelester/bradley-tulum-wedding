Tulum has some marvelous food and drinks. From world class pasta to classic street tacos, you can find anything and everything in Tulum. Here's some of our favorite restaurant suggestions.

grab a drink

You can grab a drink pretty much anywhere in Tulum but here's some of our favorite nightlife spots -- Gitano has a world class mezcal bar with DJ's spinning certain nights of the week. Casa Jaguar brings some great outdoor vibes making it a great restaurant-bar to grab a drink at. Papaya Playa Project has different DJ's almost every night of the week and is a great beachclub with nightlife. While Batey's isn't the biggest or best bar it's got some killer mojito's.

Gitano

Casa Jaguar

Papaya Playa Club

Batey Mojito Bar

breakfast

Breakfast is my personal favorite meal of the day in Tulum. Most breakfast are super healthy, full of fresh fruit and produce. Check out Mi Amor, La Zebra or Casa Malca for a traditional resort breakfast with a cheeky glass of Champagne. Matcha Mama is a smoothie and acai bar in the middle of the resort zone with great breakfast options. Arrive early & start your day off right with a great breakfast!

Mi Amor

Casa Malca

La Zebra

Matcha Mama

lunch

I think the best way to eat lunch in Tulum is to check out the many beach club menu's. They have great options for food while you're lounging on the beach. Some of our favorites are Coco Tulum which has traditional Mexican and Mezzanine which has lovely Thai food, both right on the beach. Burrito Amor is also a staple in Tulum, obviously serving burritos. Humo has a great atmosphere, drinks, shareable plates and floor seating just outside of town.

Humo

Coco Tulum

Mezzanine

Burrito Amor

nice dinner

If you want to splurge on a nice dinner with a good atmophere check out one of these restaurants! Hartwood is considered one of the best restaurants in Tulum and has a rotating menu based on what's currently fresh in the area. Posada Margherita has incredible Italian food right on the beach. Arca and Bal Nak are outdoor restuarants with great vibes in the resort zone.

Hartwood

Posada Margherita

Arca

Bal Nak

cheap eats

Tulum doesn't have to be expensive! There's plenty of authentic local restaurants and food trucks that are great value for money. With the peso around 22 to $1 you can get your money's worth easily. Here's some cheap eats around town if you're balling on a budget.

Uno Japanese Noodles

Palma Central Food Trucks

Tropi-Q

El Camello Tulum

vegetarian & vegan

Calling all plant based & healthy humans! Tulum has lots of vegetarian and vegan options. Raw Love Tulum is located inside of a very cool resort surrounded by modern art. Macondo is a holistic eatery also in one of Tulum's best resorts and features Moroccan style seating. Laylo is an great vegan lounge in town. Charly's is one of the best ranked vegan restuarants in town with a cool atmosphere!

Raw Love Tulum

Macondo at Nomade

Laylo
