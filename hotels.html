If you'd like to stay directly on the beach in the middle of the resort zone, picking a hotel is for you! It's a bit more expensive than staying in town but can be great if you'd like to be walking or biking distance from everything you need.

luxury hotels

These hotels range from normally $400-$1000 a night but are well worth the funds. Most are eco-luxury which means they are built from the land around them and use green technologies to exist without harming their natural environment. Notable mentions also include Casa Malca, La Zebra, La Valise Tulum, Ahau Tulum & Sanara Tulum.

mid-level hotels

These hotels range from normally $150-$350 a night and are also located on the beach. They're great options if you want the luxury of being right on the beach without paying an high price. Notable mentions include: Amansala, Kore Tulum Retreat, My Way Boutique Hotel, Rosa del Viento & Diamante K.

economic hotels & hostels

These hotels and hostels range from typically $15-150 a night and are located around Tulum. They're great options if you want to save money, meet new people and are traveling solo or in a small group. Some of these offer free breakfast and bikes. Check out Hostelworld for more options!
